Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer home delivery services?

Yes, we offer home delivery services within Bangalore city. For orders outside Bangalore, we use courier services.

What are your payment methods?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers.

How long does it take to complete a custom order?

The time required depends on the complexity of the design and our current workload. Typically, it takes 7-10 days for custom orders.

Do you offer alterations for purchased items?

Yes, we offer alteration services for items purchased from our store. Minor alterations are done free of charge.
